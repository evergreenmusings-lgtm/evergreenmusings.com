This page explains what information Evergreen Musings collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. This site is a personal blog, so the amount of data collected here is intentionally small.

Information I Collect

If you fill out a contact or comment form, the information you submit (such as your name and email address) is sent through Formspree, a third-party form service, so I can read and respond to your message. I don't collect this information unless you choose to submit it.

Cookies

This site uses cookies to support basic functionality and, where applicable, to remember your cookie consent preferences (via Cookieyes). You can manage or withdraw your consent at any time through the cookie settings link in the site footer.

Images

Images on this site are hosted through Cloudinary, a third-party media hosting service. Loading these images may involve standard server logs (such as IP address) collected by Cloudinary as part of delivering content.

Advertising & Affiliate Links

Some posts may contain affiliate links, which means I may earn a small commission if you purchase something through them, at no extra cost to you. This site may also display ads through Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites on the internet. This allows Google and its partners to show you ads that may be more relevant to your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or visit aboutads.info to opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for interest-based advertising.

Third-Party Services

This site relies on the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • GitHub Pages (site hosting)
  • Formspree (contact form handling)
  • Cloudinary (image hosting)
  • Cookieyes (cookie consent management)
  • Google AdSense (advertising, if enabled)

Your Choices

You can browse this site without submitting any personal information. If you've previously contacted me and would like your information removed, just reach out and I'll take care of it.
