About

Hey! I’m Evelyn, a college student - turned creative writer- interested in movies, tv shows, books, poetry, writing, art, you name it! I love any product of expression that allows us to convey stories and ideas with meaning.

A couple years ago, I was halted by my words. The thoughts, the ideas, the imaginations... I couldn't find the exact words to express them. And so they would pass as they were observed.

Expression is the medium to capture our presence. To represent stories- to represent lives. To dissociate and observe our mind for what it produces and to convey these productions with the same intensity they come on with.

It's not simple. We could write novel after novel to emphasize a core idea yet the interpretation may never capture the true essence we intend.

I’m here to tell you, you don’t have to write a novel!

Short forms of writing can still capture a lot of meaning. Short stories, poems, journal entries, blog posts, and one- act plays are valuable forms of writing when you can’t commit to a novel.

Expression should be accessible, and I’m here to help make it that way.

I created Evergreen Musings as a platform to help people express themselves and their ideas through short forms of creative and personal writing. Whether you are a beginner writer or an advanced storyteller, I hope this website could inspire you to articulate your thoughts with presence.
