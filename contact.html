About

I started writing because I didn't know what else to do with all the thoughts that didn't belong anywhere else. They were too quiet for conversation, too personal for small talk — so I gave them a page.

Evergreen Musings is a space I built slowly, the way you'd arrange a room you plan to live in for a long time. Nothing rushed. Nothing for show. Just ideas I wanted to return to, and words that felt worth keeping.

I write about the films that stay with me, the journals I keep, the small rituals that make ordinary days feel intentional. Sometimes I write about writing itself — the mess of it, the quiet satisfaction when something finally clicks.

If you've found your way here, I hope something on these pages feels like it was written with you in mind. Because in some way, it was.
